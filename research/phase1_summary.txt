KEY FINDINGS
- The BIG win is the training recipe (lr=1e-3 + grad clipping + LR schedule with decay).
  - p1H (just lr=1e-3 + grad_clip=0.5 + cosine) gets within 7 pts of full T++ (113 vs 106)
  - p1A (original lr=5e-4) is 32 pts worse than p1D
- Eidetic alone is WORSE than baseline (148 vs 138) — only helps when combined with high LR
- Global conditioning alone is barely better than baseline (140 vs 138)
- Slice_num=16 is faster but converges similar quality (118)
- N_head=8 (with hidden=128, dim_head=16) is mediocre
- Surf_weight=20 alone doesn't help much
